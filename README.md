## 🍫 World Chocolate Sales Dashboard (2023–2024)
This project is a fully interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing global chocolate sales performance across brands, countries, demographics, and store types.
This project demonstrates my strong skills in data cleaning, DAX, data modeling, and insight storytelling.

The data used here was taken from Kaggle website, and here's a link to the website: [kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ssssws/chocolate-sales-dataset-2023-2024).


## 📌 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of chocolate sales from January 2023 to December 2024, enabling stakeholders to understand:
-	Profitability across brands and countries

-	Customer demographics and purchasing behavior

-	Store type performance

-	Year over year profit trends

-	Product category contribution

The goal of this project is to help business leaders identify growth opportunities, high performing markets, and strategic focus areas. On a personal terms, this project is otherwise used to show my strength in using Power Bi. 


## 🎯 Key Business Questions
During this project, the key questions that I was interested in answering are listed below:

-	Which brands generate the highest profit?

-	Which countries contribute most to revenue and profit?

-	How do store types differ in sales volume?

-	What is the yearly profit trend?

-	Which customer segments drive the most sales?

-	Which product categories perform best?

## 📈 Key Insights

**🏆 1. Brand Performance**
-	**Ferrero** leads with $1.88M profit.

-	**Cadbury and Lindt** follow closely with $1.87M and $1.77M.

-	All six major brands show strong profitability, indicating a competitive market.

![brand_trends](https://github.com/abdulaikabba19/chocolate_sales_PBI/blob/main/image/profit_by_brand.png?raw=true)

*This bar chart illustrates how the total profit is made by brands*

**🌍 2. Country-Level Profit & Revenue**

-	**Canada** is the top-performing market with $2.0M profit and $5.1M revenue.

-	**UK, USA, and France** show similar performance levels.

-	**Germany** has the lowest profit among the six countries analyzed.

![country_trend](https://github.com/abdulaikabba19/chocolate_sales_PBI/blob/main/image/profit&revenue_by_country.png?raw=true)

*This chart illustrates how revenue and profit is made in the different countries*

**🏪 3. Store-Type Sales**

-	**Airport** stores dominate with 896K units sold — likely due to high tourist traffic.

-	**Mall and Online** channels also perform strongly.

-	**Retail** stores show the lowest volume, suggesting room for improvement.
